Welcome to "Sant Shiromani Guru Ravidas and Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Shiksha Samaj Kalyan Yuva Sangathan Sameeti"

Brief description of the organization (Committee)- The committee was established or built in very adverse circumstances. In the year 2015, the birth anniversary could not happen, this time also no one was ready to do the birth anniversary. We went to everyone but nobody was ready. We went to Shri Rishipal, Shri Mangeram, Shri Munna, Shri Pradeep, Shri Bablu, Shri Prakash, Shri Raju, Shri Lal Singh, Shri Jasbir, Shri Yashpal, Shri Ishrapal, Shri Pahal Singh, all of them, but all said that work is not going on for us. Then the next day he went to Parle Chamaro for a surprise but he refused to give it. Ravindra Kumar (Champi chamar) collected the boys and provided detailed information to all. The committee was established on 7th February, 2016 on a Sunday.

The committee was named - Sant Shiromani Guru Ravidas and Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Sahab ji Social Welfare Youth Organization. The committee consisted of 5 boys, including Ravinder Kumar s/o Karam singh (President), Sachin Kumar 0/s Rishipal (vice President), Ravi Chauhan (Secretary), Vikas kumar s/o Kartar singh (Manager) and Arun s/o Palu (Treasurer). Thus the committee was constituted. The main objective of the committee is social welfare and to help the poor people. The society can be developed by the worker and help the people in every possible way. The ultimate objective of the committee is based on the Begumpura country of Sant Shiromani Guru Ravidas. In such a wish, I get food for everyone. Small and big all settled down, Ravidas remained happy. and is based on the Preamble of the Constitution of India. In this way, the committee has many objectives which are based on the ideology of Santguru Ravidas ji and the Indian Constitution for which the committee has been set up.
